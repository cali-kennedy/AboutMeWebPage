Project ShowCase

Go Study! – A Study-Based RPG Game in Java

Go Study! is an educational RPG game developed in Java, inspired by the classic Pokémon game structure. The game is designed to make studying more engaging by integrating study questions into gameplay mechanics. Players can explore a top-down map, encounter enemies, and engage in turn-based battles where the correctness of their answers to study questions influences the outcome of battles and the experience points (XP) they earn.
